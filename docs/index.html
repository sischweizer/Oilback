Our Mission

At OILBACK, we reduce waste by reclaiming and reusing cutting fluids from metal chips through our innovative mobile recycling service.

Our mission is to ensure eco-efficiency and financial savings for manufacturers by providing sustainable solutions directly at their production sites.

The Problem

The metal manufacturing industry generates significant waste in the form of oily metal chips. Manufacturers face high costs in disposing of this waste through inefficient methods, harming both finances and the environment.

Our Solution

Mobile Recycling Service

We separate oil from metal chips directly at the manufacturer's site, enabling them to reuse the oil and efficiently recycle the metal. This faster, cheaper, and sustainable method helps reduce environmental impact and costs.
